# Experimental Imaging Centre

The Experimental Imaging Centre (EIC) is a preclinical imaging core facility at the University of Calgary, located in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. 

EIC operations are centered around a 11.7T Bruker preclinical MRI system (equipped with a cryo-cooled RF coil), and are supported by a heterogeneous computational platform consisting of servers, high performance workstations, and service nodes. 

This is the EIC's public software repository.
